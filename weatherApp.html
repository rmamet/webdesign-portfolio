Weather App

Web Design II Project

Originally submitted on Nov. 18, 2025

This Weather App was made to be a "fun and original" app. By hitting https://weather.gov's API, I am able to get the weather for any location in the United States of America. To determine "Should you wear a shirt", it checks (in order) the Probability of Precipitation, the Temperature, the Relative Humidity, and if it is Daytime. This was a really good refresher on APIs other than the one that I made.
